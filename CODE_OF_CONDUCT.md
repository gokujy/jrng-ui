# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

JRNG UI contributors and maintainers commit to a respectful, harassment-free, and constructive community. Be considerate, discuss technical decisions with evidence, accept correction, and make participation welcoming regardless of identity, background, experience, or ability.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sustained disruption, publishing private information, and unwanted sexual attention.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through GitHub. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, issue warnings, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban participants when necessary. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project publicly.
